the scientific legacy of ancient greece ancient greece witnessed a golden age of scientific inquiry and mathematical innovation the contribution made by greek thinker during this period laid the groundwork for much of the scientific knowledge we posse today one of the most influential figure in greek mathematics wa euclid his work element became the definitive textbook on geometry and remained the standard for over two millennium euclid systematic approach to proof and geometric principle shaped the study of mathematics for century to come his work not only provided a comprehensive framework for understanding geometry but also introduced the concept of axiom and logical reasoning greek astronomer also made significant stride in understanding the cosmos aristarchus of samos proposed a heliocentric model of the universe with the sun at the center and the earth and other planet revolving around it this groundbreaking idea wa overshadowed by the prevailing geocentric model proposed by ptolemy but laid the foundation for future debate on the nature of the solar system in addition to mathematics and astronomy ancient greek scientist made advancement in the field of medicine and biology hippocrates often referred to a the father of medicine developed the hippocratic oath and emphasized the importance of observing patient symptom and creating a detailed medical history his approach to medicine focused on understanding the human body a a complex system which greatly influenced the development of western medicine
