sport and event at the olympic game the olympic game were primarily focused on athletic competition and participant from various greek city state would gather to showcase their physical prowess the most prestigious event wa the stadion race which wa held on the first day of the game and attracted the most attention other track event included the diaulos a two stade race the hoplitodromos a race in full armor and the dolichos a long distance race these race provided an opportunity for athlete to demonstrate their speed endurance and agility apart from track event the olympic game featured a wide range of other sport the pentathlon a combination of five event including long jump discus throw javelin throw stadion race and wrestling wa one of the most challenging and revered competition additionally there were combat sport like boxing and pankration which wa a mix of boxing and wrestling equestrian event such a chariot racing and horseback riding were also included in the game
