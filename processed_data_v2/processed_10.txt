the rise of city state athens and sparta introduction to ancient greece ancient greece is often regarded a the cradle of western civilization and it rich history and culture have fascinated scholar and enthusiast for century the period of ancient greece spanned from the archaic period in the 8th century bce to the hellenistic period in the 4th century bce one of the most interesting aspect of ancient greece is the development of city state which played a significant role in shaping the political social and cultural landscape of the region among the most influential city state were athens and sparta which emerged a powerful and distinct entity during this era this chapter will explore the rise of these city state shedding light on their unique characteristic political system and military prowess
