olympic game athletic competition in ancient greece introduction to the olympic game the olympic game were one of the most iconic and celebrated ritual in ancient greece these athletic competition were held every four year in olympia a small town in the western part of the peloponnese peninsula the game were dedicated to zeus the king of the greek god and were considered a major religious event this chapter will delve into the origin of the olympic game the various sporting event and the significance of these competition in ancient greek society origin and history of the olympic game the exact origin of the olympic game are shrouded in myth and legend according to ancient greek mythology the game were founded by hercules a a tribute to his father zeus however historical evidence suggests that the first olympic game took place in 776 bce initially the game consisted of only a single event a footrace called the stadion measuring approximately 192 meter over time more event were added and the game evolved into a multi day festival
